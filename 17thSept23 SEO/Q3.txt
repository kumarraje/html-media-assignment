Using meta tags in HTML offers several benefits:

Improved Search Visibility: Meta tags help search engines understand your content, leading to better ranking in search results.

Higher Click-Through Rates: A well-written meta description can attract users to click on your link, increasing traffic.

Keyword Optimization: Meta keywords offer some keyword context, aiding in search engine indexing.

Consistent Branding: Meta tags maintain consistency in branding, attributing content to specific authors or contributors.

Enhanced Usability: Meta viewport tag ensures proper rendering across devices, improving user experience.

Structured Data for Rich Snippets: Structured data markup enables rich snippets in search results, making listings more informative and appealing.
